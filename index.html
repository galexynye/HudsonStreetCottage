About the Hudson Street Cottage

Built in 1920 and located on one of the most sought after streets in all of Hudson, the Hudson Street Cottage is within walking distance to downtown Hudson and Hudson City schools.

It’s private back yard backs up to the Western Reserve athletic fields and hiking trails. Perfect for hikes and dog walking.

The inside consists of 3 bedrooms and 2.5 baths, with a rec room in the lower level that could function as a guest room. The kitchen has been updated with granite countertops and opens to a sun room and casual dining area with slate floor over looking the back yard gardens and fields of WRA.

Beautiful hardwood floors in all the bedrooms and many period details that make you feel like you’re living in the English countryside.

The sellers are also including their architectural plans for future renovations and additions, all approved by the zoning board of the city of Hudson.
